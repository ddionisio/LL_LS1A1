Perfect Cell

Journey into the depths to grow and evolve as the perfect cell. Keep moving forward!

Controls:
Use the touchpad or mouse for interactions.

Camera Movement - You can use the arrow keys or WASD to move the camera, or drag with the mouse.
Camera Zoom In/Out - Press the +/- sign found above the timer bar at the bottom. You can also press Q/E or +/- on the keyboard.
Time Skip - While playing, you can speed up the time by pressing the button found at the bottom of the screen, left of the timer bar.
Environment Select/Organism Edit - While playing, if you want to select a new environment or modify the organism, you can do so by pressing the buttons found at the bottom right of the screen.
